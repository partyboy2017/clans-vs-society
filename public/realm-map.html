The Realm of Karthûl
Once a unified kingdom, Karthûl fractured long ago into a scattering of distant lands — each shaped by its own fortune and ruin. The Eldric Kingdoms remain the beating heart of what civilization still stands, while frozen wastes, sunken swamps, burning peaks, and drifting isles mark the realm's far edges. Every lord who rises here does so on the bones of what came before. Most of these lands are still being charted — more will open to travel as the realm grows.
