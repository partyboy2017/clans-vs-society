The War-Censer (13 Names)

Massive, heavy metal incense burners swung on chains to spread blessed vapors while shattering bones.

    The Thurible of Wrath (Classic liturgical name / Aggressive)

    The Smoking Homily (Preaching through fire and ash)

    The Dawn-Bringer’s Flambeau (Solar light / Thick white smoke)

    The Ashen Liturgy (Chanted prayers manifested as choking smoke)

    The Shroud of Piety (Blinding, protective clouds for allies)

    The Heretic’s Lung (Suffocating, caustic smoke for sinners)

    The Pendulum of Grace (Rhythmic, swinging judgment)

    The Smoldering Creed (Unending, burning faith)

    The Vapor of Absolution (Forgiveness through purifying smoke)

    The Sanctuary Ember (Carrying the literal fire of the home altar into battle)

    The Blind Zealot (Fills the area with thick smoke to blind enemies)

    The Incense of Judgement (A heavy, brass burner etched with commandments)

    The Holy Reek (Pungent, supernatural aroma that repels fiends and undead)
