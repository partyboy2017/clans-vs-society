The Realm of Karthûl
Once a unified kingdom, Karthûl fractured long ago — its crown lost, its treasury scattered, its people divided into warring clans. What remains is a land of ruin and opportunity: forests thick with bandits, marshes that hide older things, mountain ruins that guard forgotten wealth, and a Rift at the world's edge that should never have opened. Every lord who rises here does so on the bones of what came before.
